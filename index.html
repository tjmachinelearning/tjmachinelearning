Welcome to TJHSST Machine Learning!

We are a group of students from Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology that are interested in machine learning. We would like to go beyond what is offered in TJ courses and help students learn more advanced and powerful tools in the field of artificial intelligence. Taking this knowledge, we apply it to practical real world problems to practice with actual data and see how we can take what we know outside the classroom.

What We Do

TJHSST Machine Learning Club aims to bring the complex and vast topic of machine learning to high school students. We teach a variety of topics, including Neural Networks, Convolutional Neural Networks, SVM's, GPU Acceleration, and more.

Competitions and Challenges

We hold challenges every week to supplement the topics we cover in the lectures. Every so often, we have larger competitions, where everyone competes to produce the best solution to solve real world challenges using actual data. TJHSST Machine Learning also strongly encouurages students to take part in external competitions to improve their knowledge.
